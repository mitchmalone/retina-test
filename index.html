This test is designed to test your MacBook Pro with Retina screen for image retention.

Instructions

  1. Open this URL in full screen mode in your browser. For best results use Firefox since it will fill the entire screen.
  2. Choose a duration and click "begin test."
  3. the grid to display for 3 minutes and the countdown to expire. IMPORTANT NOTE: Since human eyes are easily tricked and actually have a form of "image retention" of their own you should not stare at the screen or watch it too closely.
  4. After 3 minutes the screen will display black, you should then inspect the screen and see if the checker pattern is still visible.
